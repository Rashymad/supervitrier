# Book Your TV Mounting Service Online

Easily schedule your TV mounting appointment in just a few steps.  
Select your desired services, choose a convenient date and time, and provide your contact details—all online!

---

## How Our Booking System Works

1. **Choose Your Services**  
   Select from wall mounting, ceiling mounting, complete setup, and more.  
   Each service includes professional installation and cable management.

2. **Select Date & Time**  
   Pick your preferred appointment date and available time slot.  
   Emergency same-day service is available for an additional surcharge.

3. **Enter Your Information**  
   Provide your contact details and service address.  
   Our system will verify your service area automatically.

4. **Review & Confirm**  
   Check your booking summary, pricing breakdown, and agree to our terms.  
   A 25% deposit secures your appointment.

5. **Receive Confirmation**  
   You’ll get a booking reference, confirmation email, and SMS updates.  
   Our technician will contact you before arrival.
